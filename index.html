Notebook 03

Sentiment intelligence

We developed a novel community-weighted satisfaction scoring system (TWSI) that incorporates engagement signals beyond raw text polarity.

This approach fundamentally altered platform rankings — surfacing community consensus as a distinct, powerful signal.

🏅

Copilot achieved the highest community-weighted score

Invisible through standard sentiment — only surfaces when community endorsement is weighted into scoring.

📈

Community signals reshape platform rankings entirely

Platforms with high raw ratings but low engagement ranked significantly lower after TWSI weighting.

📉

Score variance exposes hidden quality risks

Boxplot analysis revealed extreme outliers in high-median platforms — a consistency gap invisible to averages.
